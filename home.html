About Us

Meet the Niagara College – Toronto (NCT) team!

FAQ

Niagara College Toronto Hackathon 2025
Learn More: https://www.niagaracollegetoronto.ca/

Empowering Innovation, Encouraging Collaboration, Building the Future.

Explore our FAQ to find answers to your questions.

What is NCT Hackathon 2025?

NCT Hackathon 2025 is a coding and innovation event where students collaborate to build solutions for real-world problems within a limited time.

Who can participate in NCT Hackathon?

The hackathon is open to students from all backgrounds, whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer.

Where will NCT Hackathon be held?

The event will take place at Niagara College Toronto campus, with online participation options available.

How do I register for NCT Hackathon?

You can register on our official website by filling out the registration form before the deadline.

What are the themes and challenges?

Themes include AI, cybersecurity, fintech, sustainability, and more. Detailed challenges will be announced closer to the event.

What should I bring to the Hackathon?

Bring your laptop, chargers, ID, and any tools or resources you may need for development.

Will there be food and refreshments?

Yes, meals and snacks will be provided throughout the event.

Do I need a team to participate?

You can register as an individual or as a team. Teams can be formed at the event as well.
